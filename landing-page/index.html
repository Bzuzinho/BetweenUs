Frequently asked questions

Is BetweenUs only for couples?

No. BetweenUs welcomes verified adults joining individually or as a couple.

How does privacy work?

You control profile visibility, photo access and who can contact you.

How is BetweenUs different?

Compatibility, consent and privacy are core product rules, not optional extras.

Is there a free version?

The beta will include free access, with optional premium privacy tools later.

How do I join the beta?

Submit your email below. Invitations will be released gradually by region.

Is my data safe?

Security measures include encrypted transport, private controls and audited moderation.

Can I delete my account?

Yes. Account deletion and data export are available from your settings.

Are photos shared publicly?

No. You choose whether photos are public, blurred or private.

What if I feel uncomfortable?

You can block, report or leave a conversation immediately using Safe Exit controls.

How does matching work?

Matching considers shared intentions, limits, preferences and mutual consent.
